# Welcome to Project Zero

Project Zero is a Prime Broker built in top of Solana, enabling users to collaterallize assets from
different venues and borrow against them. Project Zero is built on top of MarginLendv2, a
battle-tested borrow-lending protocol with powerful embedded fully on-chain risk systems.


## Key Features

* Lend collateral to earn yield

* Borrow against your collateral to maximize capital efficiency and take advantage of arbitrage opportunities.

* Comprehensive access to all liquidity: no fragmented or isolated pools.

* Enjoy Emode benefits across favorable asset pairings, maximizing borrowing power on correlated assets.

* Flashloans to take advantage of instant market opportunities that can be realized in a single tx.

* Loop lending and borrowing atomically to maximize leverage.
